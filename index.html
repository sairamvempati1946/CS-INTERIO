Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our interior design services

The cost varies based on project scope, materials, and customization. Typically, residential interiors range from ₹800-₹2,500 per sq.ft., while commercial spaces range from ₹1,200-₹3,500 per sq.ft. We offer customized quotes after understanding your requirements.

Our 4-step process includes: 1) Initial consultation & site visit, 2) Concept development & 3D visualization, 3) Material selection & detailed planning, 4) Execution with quality checks. We maintain regular communication throughout.

Timeline depends on project size: 2BHK apartments take 45-60 days, 3BHK apartments take 60-90 days, and commercial spaces take 90-120 days. We provide detailed timelines in our project schedule.

We use premium, durable materials including: BWP grade plywood, waterproof MDF, premium laminates, hardware from Hafele/Godrej, and trusted paint brands. We provide material samples for client approval.

Yes! We offer flexible payment plans: 20% advance, 40% after material approval, 30% during execution, and 10% upon completion. EMI options are available through our finance partners.
